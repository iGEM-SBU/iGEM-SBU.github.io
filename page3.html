Collaboration with Queens iGEM (QGem)
 Both of our teams were interested in performing a type of directed evolution (DE) for our projects. However, there weren’t that many clear resources on DE and how to perform it. We teamed up to produce a short video and a pamphlet for future iGEM Teams. We worked together on the research, script, and pamphlet. QGem recorded the video, and Stony Brook iGEM edited the video and added additional graphics. We hope that future teams can use these resources as a starting point for implementing DE into their projects.
